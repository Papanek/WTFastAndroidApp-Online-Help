Graphing your upload and download bandwidths!

Tap the Graph Bandwidth button
to enter the graphing screen.
The graphing screen, tap the
Start Graphing button to begin.

Tap the Relative button to
graph relative to the highest
point on the graph.
Tap the Absolute button to
graph withing set scaled
increments.

To stop graphing click the
Stop Graphing button.
