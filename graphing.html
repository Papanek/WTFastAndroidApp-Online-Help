Graphing your bandwidths!

Tap the Graph Bandwidth button
to enter the graphing screen.
Tap the play button
to begin graphing.

Tap the "Relative" button
to set the graph relative
to the highest point.
Tap the Absolute button to
graph within set scaled
increments.

To stop graphing click
the pause button.



Tap the gear icon in the
top right to select from
all of the graphing
options!
Fullscreen & Landscape

Turn your device sideways to display
the graph in landscape mode.

Tap graphing area on the screen
to begin graphing in fullscreen!
