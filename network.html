Viewing your router's information!

Tap the View Network Infomation
button to view your router's info.


You will be prompted for your
router's admin password
to proceed.

Tap the Save Password
checkbox to hold your
password for the future
The name, uptime, IP address,
and connected devices of
your router will be shown.
Error Messages

If a connection to the given
IP address of your router is
not made, a message will be
shown to notify you.
