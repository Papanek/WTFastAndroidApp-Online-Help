Setup Graphing Viewing Information Rebooting

After you click on the Network Information button
you will be prompted for your router's
admin password to proceed.
The Network Information box will then be displayed.
Your router information, ip, uptime, and all
connected devices to your router will be shown.

If a connection to the given IP address of your
router is not made, a "Connection Error"
message will be shown to notify you.
