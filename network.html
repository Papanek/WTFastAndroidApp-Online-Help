Viewing your router's information!

Tap the View Network Infomation
button to view your router's info.

You will be prompted for your router's
admin password to proceed.
Your router's name, IP, uptime, and
connected devices will be shown.
Error Messages

If a connection to the given IP address
of your router is not made, a message
will be shown to notify you.
