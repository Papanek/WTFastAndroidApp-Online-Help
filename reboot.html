Rebooting your router!

Tap the Reboot Router button
to reboot your router.
Enter in your router's admin
password to proceed.

A message will be shown to
notify you that your router
is rebooting.
A notification will be shown
after a successful reboot.
Error Messages

If a connection to the given
IP address of your router is
not made, a message will be
shown to notify you.
