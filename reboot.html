Rebooting your router!

Tap the Reboot Router button
to reboot your router.
To reboot your router enter in your
router's admin password.

A message will be shown to notify you
that your router is rebooting.
A message will be shown after your
router has successfully rebooted!
Error Messages

If a connection to the given IP address
of your router is not made, a message
will be shown to notify you.
